About the Animals

CATS
- The cat (Felis catus) is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal.
DOGS
- The domestic dog or simply dog (Canis familiaris when considered a distinct species or Canis lupus familiaris
when considered a subspecies of the wolf) is a member of the genus Canis (canines), which forms part of the
wolf-like canids, and is the most widely abundant terrestrial carnivore.
